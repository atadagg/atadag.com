GreenPass

GreenPass

Sustainable flight rewards app for eco-conscious travelers

View on GitHub View Presentation
React Native Hackathon Sustainability

About the Project

GreenPass is a mobile application developed for a hackathon where we reached the finals. The app encourages sustainable travel by rewarding eco-conscious flight choices. Key features include:

  • Sustainable Flights: Choose flights with lower carbon footprints to protect the environment.
  • EcoPoint System: Earn rewards for environmentally friendly choices.
  • User Profile: Track your sustainability level and earn badges.
  • PNR Integration: Add your ticket information to earn EcoPoints.
  • Level Up: Choose eco-friendly flights to earn EcoPoints, spend them in the store, and increase your benefits.
  • Use Rewards: Enjoy lounge access, fast track passage, and special gifts to enhance your travel experience.
